# 分集示例

基于霸总/复仇类小说的分集改编示例。

## 示例1：订婚晚宴冲突（原小说第一章）

### 原文概要
- 订婚宴上，女主因酒精过敏以茶代酒
- 实习生（小三）故意找茬，泼茶羞辱
- 男主（未婚夫）不帮女主，反而要女主道歉
- 女主哥哥霸气现身护妹
- 揭露女主真实身份是顶级家族大小姐

### 分集方案

#### EP1: "Engagement Party Disaster" (2:00)
**Hook**: 茶被泼出的瞬间
**Climax**: 女主被羞辱，男主不帮
**Cliffhanger**: 神秘男人出现

```
[SCENE 1 - 0:00-0:05] HOOK
Visual: Wine glass knocked over, tea splashing
SFX: Glass breaking sound
CHLOE: "How dare you toast with tea?!"

[SCENE 2 - 0:05-0:25] SETUP
Visual: Wide shot of elegant party
STELLA: "I have an allergy—"
CHLOE: "Excuses! Drink up!"
ETHAN: (cold) "She's right. Apologize."

[SCENE 3 - 0:25-1:00] CONFLICT
Visual: Ethan grabs Stella's wrist
STELLA: (shakes him off) "It's our engagement day."
CHLOE: (fake crying) "She bullies me..."
ETHAN: "Apologize to Chloe! Now!"
STELLA: (cold smile) "Mind your own business."

[SCENE 4 - 1:00-1:30] CLIMAX
Visual: Ethan raises hand to slap Stella
STELLA: (unflinching) "Do it. We're done."
CHLOE: (smirking) "Finally showing your true colors."

[SCENE 5 - 1:30-2:00] CLIFFHANGER
Visual: Hand grabs Ethan's wrist from behind
ALEXANDER: "How dare you hit her?"
[FREEZE on Alexander's cold eyes]
TEXT: "TO BE CONTINUED"
```

#### EP2: "The Mysterious Protector" (1:30)
**Hook**: 哥哥护妹的霸气场面
**Climax**: 震慑全场
**Cliffhanger**: 身份暗示

```
[SCENE 1 - 0:00-0:05] HOOK
Visual: Alexander's hand crushing Ethan's wrist
ALEXANDER: "I said... how dare you?"

[SCENE 2 - 0:05-0:25] RISING
Visual: Ethan in pain, shocked
ETHAN: "Who are you?!"
ALEXANDER: (releases him) "Someone you don't want to mess with."
CHLOE: (whispering) "Why is he defending her?"

[SCENE 3 - 0:25-1:00] CONFLICT
Visual: Alexander helps Stella up gently
ALEXANDER: "Sister, are you hurt?"
GASP from crowd
CHLOE: "S-sister?!"
ETHAN: (pale) "What did you call her?"

[SCENE 4 - 1:00-1:30] CLIFFHANGER
Visual: Alexander turns to face everyone
ALEXANDER: "Touch her again... and you'll face me."
[Close up of Alexander's dangerous smile]
TEXT: "WHO IS HE?"
```

#### EP3: "The Truth Revealed" (2:30)
**Hook**: 哥哥宣布重大消息
**Climax**: 女主真实身份揭露
**Cliffhanger**: 男主后悔莫及

```
[SCENE 1 - 0:00-0:10] HOOK
Visual: Alexander addressing the room
ALEXANDER: "I have an announcement."
ETHAN: (nervous) "What now?"

[SCENE 2 - 0:10-0:40] SETUP
Visual: Alexander's authoritative stance
ALEXANDER: "The Gray family cuts all ties with Foster Corp."
CROWD: (gasping)
ETHAN: (panicking) "You can't! We're partners!"

[SCENE 3 - 0:40-1:30] CLIMAX
Visual: Alexander reveals Stella's identity
ALEXANDER: "This is Stella Gray. My sister. Heiress to Gray Group."
[FLASHBACK: Stella working, being humble]
[BACK TO: Present, everyone's shocked faces]
CHLOE: (falling back) "Impossible..."

[SCENE 4 - 1:30-2:00] FALLING
Visual: Ethan kneeling, begging
ETHAN: "Stella, I didn't know! Forgive me!"
STELLA: (cold) "You chose her. Remember?"

[SCENE 5 - 2:00-2:30] CLIFFHANGER
Visual: Chloe desperate, pulling out phone
CHLOE: "Wait! I have proof she's a fraud!"
[Shows photo]
TEXT: "WHAT'S THE SECRET?"
```

## 示例2：回忆与真相（原小说第3章）

### 原文概要
- 回忆五年前初遇
- 女主帮男主创业
- 男主忘恩负义
- 分手决定

### 分集方案

#### EP4: "Memories of Betrayal" (2:00)
**Hook**: 回忆闪回
**Climax**: 回忆 vs 现实对比
**Cliffhanger**: 分手宣言

```
[SCENE 1 - 0:00-0:05] HOOK
Visual: Flashback effect, younger Stella
YOUNG ETHAN: "Join me. Together we'll conquer the world."

[SCENE 2 - 0:05-0:30] FLASHBACK
Visual: Startup office, humble beginnings
STELLA V.O.: "Five years ago, I believed him."
[Montage: Stella working hard, securing deals]

[SCENE 3 - 0:30-1:00] BACK TO PRESENT
Visual: Bitter smile on present Stella
STELLA: "Look at us now."
ETHAN: (angry) "You've changed!"
STELLA: "No. You have."

[SCENE 4 - 1:00-1:30] CLIMAX
Visual: Ethan's cruel words
ETHAN: "You got lucky! You think you're capable?"
[FLASHBACK: Ethan's grateful face]
[BACK TO: Ethan's contemptuous face]

[SCENE 5 - 1:30-2:00] CLIFFHANGER
Visual: Stella's determined expression
STELLA: "Ethan Foster... we're done."
[Walks away without looking back]
TEXT: "IS IT REALLY OVER?"
```

## 示例3：真假千金对峙（原小说第7章）

### 分集方案

#### EP15: "The Impostor Exposed" (2:30)
**Hook**: 假千金视频通话
**Climax**: 真千金现身
**Cliffhanger**: 真相大白

```
[SCENE 1 - 0:00-0:10] HOOK
Visual: Phone screen showing video call
CHLOE: (excited) "Olivia, tell them! She's a fake!"

[SCENE 2 - 0:10-0:40] SETUP
Visual: Daisy on screen, nervous
DAISY: "Yes, she's just a housekeeper's daughter!"
ETHAN: (relieved) "I knew it!"

[SCENE 3 - 0:40-1:30] CLIMAX
Visual: Screen shakes, Olivia grabs phone
OLIVIA: "Daisy... you're wearing my clothes. My necklace."
DAISY: (shaking) "I-I can explain—"
OLIVIA: "The real question is... who is she?"
[Points to Stella]

[SCENE 4 - 1:30-2:00] REVELATION
Visual: Olivia smiles at Stella
OLIVIA: "Sister Stella! I've missed you!"
[Everyone's shocked faces]
CHLOE: (falling) "No... no..."

[SCENE 5 - 2:00-2:30] CLIFFHANGER
Visual: Alexander enters, protective
ALEXANDER: "Anyone else doubt my sister's identity?"
[Close up on defeated Ethan and Chloe]
TEXT: "THE QUEEN RETURNS"
```

## 不同场景的分集技巧

### 1. 长对话场景 → 拆成2-3集

**原文**：两人争吵持续5分钟

**改编**：
- EP8: 争吵开始 → 第一次反转
- EP9: 争吵升级 → 第二次反转
- EP10: 争吵高潮 → 决裂

### 2. 回忆场景 → 独立成集或穿插

**方式1：独立回忆集**
- EP6: "Five Years Ago" (纯回忆)

**方式2：闪回穿插**
- EP7: 当前场景
- [FLASHBACK: 5 seconds]
- 回到当前

### 3. 多线叙事 → 集内切换

```
EP12:
Scene 1: 女主线 - 准备反击
Scene 2: 反派的线 - 策划阴谋
Scene 3: 男主线 - 后悔
Scene 4: 三线交汇 - 冲突爆发
```

## 分集长度参考

| 集类型 | 时长 | 字数 | 适用场景 |
|--------|------|------|----------|
| 快速冲突 | 1:00 | 300-400 | 简单对峙 |
| 标准 | 1:30 | 450-600 | 一般情节 |
| 复杂 | 2:00 | 600-800 | 多场景/转折 |
| 特别篇 | 3:00 | 900-1200 | 重大揭秘 |
